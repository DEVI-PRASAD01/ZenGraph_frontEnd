Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to ZenGraph. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website or use our mobile application (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect

Personal data, or personal information, means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: Includes first name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Includes email address and account credentials.
  • Meditation & Wellness Data: Includes your mood history, voice check-ins, meditation session logs, and personal goals.
  • Technical Data: Includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, and other technology on the devices you use to access this app.
  • Usage Data: Includes information about how you use our app and services.

3. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To register you as a new user and manage your account.
  • To provide personalized AI-driven meditation plans and insights (AI Analysis).
  • To process voice check-ins for emotional state analysis.
  • To send you push notifications and reminders for your sessions.
  • To improve our app, services, and user experience including research and analytics.

4. AI and Voice Analysis

ZenGraph uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your mood and voice check-ins. This data is handled with extreme care:

  • Voice Recording: Brief audio clips for "Express Yourself" sessions are processed to identify emotional patterns only.
  • AI Insights: Historical mood data is used to generate personalized wellbeing reports and goals.
  • Privacy-First: We use encryption and anonymization protocols to ensure your sensitive wellness data remains secure.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your data with service providers that perform functions on our behalf, such as cloud storage (AWS/Google Cloud) and analytics providers (Firebase). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for the purposes we specify.

6. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

7. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, transfer, to object to processing, to portability of data and to withdraw consent.
